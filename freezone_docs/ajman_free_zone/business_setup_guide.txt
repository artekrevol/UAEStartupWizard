# Ajman Free Zone Business Setup Guide

Ajman Free Zone offers a range of business setup options with competitive pricing and strategic location.

## Key Benefits
- 100% foreign ownership
- 0% corporate and income tax
- No currency restrictions
- Proximity to major UAE markets
- Fast business registration process

## Business Activities
Ajman Free Zone supports various business activities including:
- Trading
- Services
- Manufacturing
- Logistics

## License Types
- Commercial License
- Service License
- Industrial License
- E-commerce License

## Requirements
- Valid passport copy
- Business plan
- Application form
- Proof of address

For more information, visit https://www.afz.ae
