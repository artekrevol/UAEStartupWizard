# Ajman Free Zone License Types Guide

Ajman Free Zone offers various license types to meet different business needs:

## Commercial License
For trading activities, import/export, and retail businesses.

## Service License
For professional and consultancy services.

## Industrial License
For manufacturing and processing activities.

## E-commerce License
For online businesses and digital platforms.

Each license type has specific requirements and fees. For detailed information, please visit https://www.afz.ae
