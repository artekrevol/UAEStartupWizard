# Ajman Free Zone Benefits Guide

## Key Benefits

### 100% Foreign Ownership
Foreign investors can own 100% of their business without a local sponsor.

### Tax Advantages
- 0% corporate tax
- 0% personal income tax
- 100% repatriation of capital and profits

### Strategic Location
- Located near Dubai and Sharjah
- Access to major UAE markets
- Proximity to ports and airports

### Cost-Effective Setup
- Competitive pricing compared to other free zones
- Flexible payment options
- Various facility options to suit different budgets

### Fast Registration Process
- Streamlined application procedures
- Quick approval process
- Minimal bureaucracy

For more information, visit https://www.afz.ae
