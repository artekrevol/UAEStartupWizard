# SAIF Zone Company Formation Process

## Formation Process
1. Select your business activity
2. Choose a legal structure (FZE, FZC, or Branch)
3. Submit name reservation application
4. Select office or warehouse facility
5. Submit required documents
6. Pay registration fees
7. Receive license and incorporation documents

## Requirements
- Minimum share capital requirements vary depending on the license type and legal structure
- At least one shareholder and one director (can be the same person)
- No prior criminal record for directors and shareholders
- Valid passport and visa for UAE residents
- Proof of address

## Required Documents
- Application form
- Passport copies of shareholders and directors
- Recent photographs
- Bank reference letter
- Business profile or plan
- Parent company documents (for branch offices)
- Board resolution (for corporate shareholders)
