# Company Formation Steps in DMCC

## Step 1: Initial Registration
- Submit company application through DMCC portal
- Pay initial registration fees
- Receive preliminary approval

## Step 2: Document Submission
- Prepare and submit all required documents
- Passport copies of all shareholders
- Business plan and company profile
- Bank reference letters

## Step 3: License Issuance
- Pay license fees
- Receive company license
- Complete company setup

## Step 4: Office Setup
- Select office space in DMCC
- Sign lease agreement
- Complete office registration

## Step 5: Visa Processing
- Apply for residence visas for owners and employees
- Complete medical tests
- Obtain Emirates ID

For more information, please contact DMCC Business Setup team.
